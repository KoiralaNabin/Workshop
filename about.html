YAMAHA

Mero Auto Workshop is an authorized service point of Yamaha Nepal established in May, 2021 located at Sundarbasti, Bhangal, Kathmandu. Mero Auto Workshop is a full- service motorcyle workshop with highly skilled and experienced mechanics providing a range of services on all types of motorcycles, including regular service, full overhauls, custom configurations, race builds and support.

The work we do:
Regular and Annual Service (see below for details) Dyno Testing and Tuning Carburetor Rebuilds, Fuel Injection Service Engine Rebuilding and Repair Transmission Rebuilding and Repair Clutch, Driveshaft and Final Drive Service and Repair Suspension Set-up, Service and Overhauls Tires, Brakes and Wheels Electrical Diagnosis and Repair Restoration Damage Estimates and Insurance Repairs Custom Work Performance and Race Work We offer maintenance packages that are broken down into three levels that should align closely with the regular service intervals for your motorcycle. Contact us for more details.
Level I :
Oil and filter change Battery check Wheel & tire inspection Cooling system check Inspect/clean air filter Lube/adjust all cables and pivot points Clean and adjust chain/belt Inspect brake system Check electrical system Inspect clutch components (external) Fastener check Set tire pressure Safety check
Level II –
Includes all of Level I in addition to: Spark plug replacement Carburetor/throttle body synchronization Inspect/change transmission fluid/driveshaft fluid Inspect steering head & wheel bearings Cooling system check & flush if needed Brake and Clutch if applicable fluid flush.
Level III –
Includes all of level I & II in addition to valve clearance inspection and adjustment as needed
